ALL ABOUT MYSELF 30 March 2019
Experiences have played a significant role in my life and acted as pivotal points in my growth trajectory since childhood. School, tuitions, and college provided me a platform to develop my skills and showcase my capabilities which further made me more ambitious towards my goals. From being a shy kid to a confident individual, my journey has been pretty marvelous and quite erratic. I demonstrated my enthusiasm and managerial potential at the age of 16 by participating in numerous sports events, inter-school competitions, and even ranked highest in mathematics. I consistently built and carried forward these traits throughout the various stages of my life. Traveling, public speaking and community services further added dimensions to my personality and made me full of gratitude and joy. On the educational front, I have participated in workshops based on basic electronics, website-development, embedded-systems etc., attended seminars on start-ups, even got a fascinating opportunity to work with some and also published a paper in an international journal. And now, I look forward to adding a new experience to my existing gamut of experiences and take a step forward towards my goal - To become a post-graduate and to continue my studies in presence of some astounding resources.

No good ending can be expected in the absence of the right beginning. Coming from the family of government employees, I had a natural inclination towards serving my country. To understand the intricacies of electrical circuits and robotics, after my high school I opted to pursue Bachelor of Technology (Electrical and Electronics Engineering), batch 2014-18 from Northern India Engineering College, Delhi and secured 76.8 % (Aggregate). During this course, I was able to sculpt myself into a whole new individual. I was exposed to various workshops and seminars after joining the IEEE society, including workshops on basic electronics, self-defence, hardware design using TI platforms, motors and drives, AutoCAD, PLC and SCADA, PCB designing, HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, events like STAR Programme and IEEE DSC, I also took part in competitions like IEEE Extreme, IEEE Technorax and won “THE BEST PRIZE” award which escalated my confidence and helped to mold myself into a productive engineer. I was able to take my creativity to another level when I got the opportunity to create my small start-up Know8 and to work with Dawnrays. Along with my other 3 friends, I started a college-socializing online organization. We created a website using ‘bootstrap’ in which all the college events were displayed along with the registration details to provide a quicker access to all students, and the individuals with some knowledge were also able to create their own events. I took 6-weeks training in “Embedded Systems” after which I was able to construct line-following robots, obstacle detector robots, quad-copter etc. I gave home tuitions to students from 7th to 10th standard during the first 3 years of college. I did my internship from Technip FMC Pvt. Ltd, Noida. The main objective of the internship was to study single-line diagrams, machinery calculations, electrical engineering activities required for distribution system of onshore terminal projects and different client documents that are required before and after the bidding procedure of a power plant project. In the final year of graduation, I got an opportunity to work on the major project “Wifi Enabled Smart Farm with Real-Time Monitoring and Energy Theft Control System”. I presented this project in an Inter-college major project competition. My research paper was published in “International Journal of Scientific and Research Publications” in their July 2018 edition.
